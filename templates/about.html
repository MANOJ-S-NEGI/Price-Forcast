About The Project

Purpose is to calculate the week-long prediction from the last date as updated in the database. Forecast Prediction Window can be customized accordingly 7 days will give optimised result

The Brent oil Price Forecasting system is designed to address the increasing need for accurate predictions of brent prices, allowing stakeholders in the energy sector to make informed decisions. By leveraging historical data, market trends, and relevant factors. The system aims to provide reliable forecasts, contributing to improved strategic planning and cost management.

Brent crude is known for its relatively low sulfur content and high API gravity, which makes it a light and sweet crude oil. Light crude oils have a lower density and flow more easily, while sweet crude oils contain less sulfur, making them less corrosive and easier to refine into high-quality products like gasoline. Brent crude is a crucial benchmark for global oil prices and serves as a reference point for pricing two-thirds of the world's internationally traded crude oil supplies. It is used in setting the price of many of the world's crude oil exports and helps determine the cost of gasoline and other refined products in various regions. The Brent crude oil price is often quoted alongside the WTI crude oil price as a key indicator of the global oil market.
